Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 15, 2026

illi is a browser extension that gives you gestural speed controls for YouTube videos. This policy explains what data illi collects, why, where it goes, and what control you have over it.

1. What Data We Collect

1.1 Data Stored on Your Device Only

The following data is stored locally using Chrome's storage API and never leaves your device unless you upgrade to a paid plan with cloud sync enabled:

  • Preferences — playback speed, auto-pause setting, mouse type, hub position
  • Channel memory — your preferred playback speed per YouTube channel (always local, never synced)
  • Bookmarks — video timestamps you've saved, stored by video ID
  • Resume positions — where you left off in videos
  • Onboarding state — whether you've completed the tutorial

1.2 Account Data

When you create an account, we collect and store:

  • Email address — used for sign-in (one-time passcode, no passwords), billing notifications, and account-related emails
  • Account metadata — subscription tier (trial, paid, founders), trial expiry date, onboarding completion flag
  • Referral data — if you use a referral link, we record which user referred you

This data is stored in our database (hosted by Supabase).

1.3 Payment Data

When you subscribe, payments are handled entirely by Stripe. We store only a Stripe customer ID and subscription ID to manage your plan status. We never receive or store your card number, billing address, or other payment details.

1.4 Cloud Sync Data (Paid Plans Only)

If you are on a paid plan, the following data is synced to our servers so it works across your devices:

  • Bookmarks — video ID, timestamp, optional label, platform
  • Preferences — playback speed, auto-pause, mouse type
  • Channel gesture settings — whether illi gestures are disabled on specific channels
  • Resume positions — last playback position per video

Channel playback speeds (your saved speed per channel) are never synced and always stay on your device.

Free trial users have no cloud-synced data. All data remains local until you subscribe.

1.5 Usage Data

We collect usage statistics to understand which features are valuable and to find bugs. This data is linked to your user ID (a random identifier, not your email) and includes:

  • Gesture events — gesture type (speed, navigation, bookmark), direction, final speed value
  • Feature interactions — which features you use (auto-pause, bookmarks)
  • Session duration — how long illi is active per page visit
  • Platform — which site you're on (e.g. "youtube"), as a single word, not a URL

Each event is sanitized before storage. Only the fields listed above are accepted; everything else is stripped both in the extension and on the server.

1.6 Error Reports

When illi crashes or encounters a bug, an error report is sent to Sentry (our error tracking service). Reports include:

  • JavaScript stack traces and error messages
  • Extension version and browser context (content script, background, or popup)
  • Platform (e.g. "youtube")
  • Your user ID (random identifier only, not your email)

Error reports do not include what you're watching, your browsing history, or your email address.

1.7 What We Do Not Collect

  • Video URLs, video titles, or any information about what you watch
  • Browsing history
  • Cookies or third-party tracking pixels
  • Device fingerprints or IP addresses (our infrastructure may log IPs at the network level, but we do not store or use them)
  • Data from any site other than YouTube

4. Your Rights and Controls

Opt Out of Usage Data

You can stop all usage data collection at any time:

  1. Click the illi hub on any YouTube page
  2. In the settings panel, toggle "Share usage data" off

When disabled, no usage events are sent to our servers. All features continue to work normally.

Access Your Data

You can request a copy of the data we hold about you by emailing us at the address in Section 9.

Delete Your Data

You can request full deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing us at the address in Section 9. Upon deletion:

  • Your profile, preferences, bookmarks, and usage data are permanently removed from our servers
  • Stripe retains billing records as required by financial regulations
  • Sentry error data expires per their retention policy (typically 90 days)

If you simply uninstall illi without requesting deletion, your local data is removed with the extension. Server-side data is retained until you request deletion.

Cancel Your Subscription

You can cancel anytime through the Stripe Customer Portal, accessible from the illi extension popup. After cancellation, your subscription remains active until the end of your current billing period, then your account transitions to an expired state.

Data Portability

If you need your data in a portable format, contact us and we will provide it within 30 days.

6. Data Security

  • All data in transit uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Cloud data is stored in Supabase's managed PostgreSQL database with Row Level Security enabled — each user can only access their own data
  • Authentication uses one-time passcodes (no passwords are stored)
  • Server-side functions use restricted API keys; the full-access service key is never exposed to the extension
  • Usage event user IDs are attached server-side from authenticated tokens, not sent from the extension, preventing spoofing

8. Children's Privacy

illi is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.
